# 8. Disassembly and Reassembly of System Units

> **WARNING:** Before disassembling any LRU, switch it off and disconnect the input electric power cable.

## 8.1 Transducer Unit Replacement
1. Locate transducers on the plate secured by four bolts.
2. Open the four bolts to remove the mounting plate from the array frame.
3. Pull the plate downward carefully to access the connectors.

## 8.3 Transceiver Unit (Internal LRUs)
To replace internal components, the **Panel PC (PPC)** must be removed first:
1. Remove the 16 bolts holding the PPC to the housing.
2. Disconnect the three cables (24 VDC, Ethernet, and Audio) connecting it to the DPGA card.

### Replacing Cards (HVPS, PA, DPGA)
1. Remove PPC (see above).
2. Disconnect cables to the specific LRU.
3. Open the mounting bolts and install the new replacement in reverse order.
