8.3	Transceiver Unit
The transceiver unit includes the following LRUs:
•	PPC
•	AC-DC power supply
•	HVPS card
•	PA card
•	DPGA card
•	Backplane
•	AC and DC power components (EMI filters, circuit breaker, contactors)
•	Fans
8.3.1	PPC replacement
To replace the PPC: 
1.	Remove the 16 bolts that hold the PPC in the transceiver housing.
2.	Pull the PPC slightly and disconnect the three cables that connect it to the DPGA card as shown in the following figure.
 
Figure 47: PPC removal
3.	Remove the PPC and install a new replacement one in the opposite sequence to its removal.
8.3.2	Removing other LRUs inside the transceiver
To remove any other LRU inside the transceiver, remove the PPC first to access the inside LRUs as shown in the following figure.
 
Figure 48: Internal LRUs of the transceiver
To replace any card (HVPS, PA, DPGA):
1.	Remove the PPC as described in section ‎8.3.1, PPC replacement, above.
2.	Disconnect the cables connected to the LRU by opening the front panel connectors.  
3.	Open the bolts that hold the LRU.
4.	Remove the LRU and install the new replacement one in the opposite sequence to its removal.
8.3.2.1	Replacing the AC_DC power supply
To replace the AC-DC power supply and the AC and DC power components:
1.	Remove the PPC as described in section ‎8.3.1, PPC replacement, above.
2.	Disconnect the cables connected to the LRU. (The cable wires are connected to the LRU by bolts.)
3.	Open the bolts that hold the LRU.
4.	Remove the LRU and install the new replacement one in the opposite sequence to its removal.
8.3.2.2	Replacing a fan
To replace a fan:
1.	Remove the PPC as described in section ‎8.3.1, PPC replacement, above.
2.	Remove the upper panel covering the fans by opening the four bolts of the upper panel.
3.	Disconnect the fan cable.
4.	Open the four bolts holding the fan to the transceiver housing ceiling.
5.	Remove the fan and install the new replacement one in the opposite sequence to its removal.
8.3.2.3	Replacing the backplane
To replace the backplane:
1.	Disconnect all cables connected to the transceiver unit rear connectors.
2.	Remove the PPC as described in section ‎8.3.1, PPC replacement, above.
3.	Remove the three LRU cards: HVPS, PA and DPGA as described in section ‎8.3.2, Removing other LRUs inside the transceiver.
4.	Remove the two fans as described in section ‎8.3.2.2, Replacing a fan.
5.	Remove any mechanical bracket that blocks the backplane.
6.	Disconnect the cables between the backplane and all remaining parts (the AC-DC power supply and the AC and DC power components).
7.	Open the four bolts holding the backplane to the transceiver housing back wall.
8.	Remove the backplane and install the new replacement one in the opposite sequence to its removal.
