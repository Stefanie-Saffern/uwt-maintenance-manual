## Distribution Box

To replace the two transformers:

1.	Disconnect the two wires of the projector from the transformer.
2.	Open all four bolts that hold the transformer in the distribution box as shown in the following figure (at the corners of the transformer).
3.	 ![Hydrophone Mounting Device](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stefanie-saffern/uwt-maintenance-manual/main/images/hardware/hydrophone-mounting-device.png)
4.	Remove the transformer and install a new replacement transformer in the opposite sequence to its removal.
