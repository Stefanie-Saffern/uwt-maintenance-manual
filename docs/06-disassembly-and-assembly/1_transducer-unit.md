# Troubleshooting and Replacement Procedures

When there is a failure in the system, the troubleshooting procedures of the previous chapter will detect the failed part, namely the line replaceable unit (LRU). The failed LRU should be replaced by a new one from the spare parts set. This chapter provides information and instructions on how to disassemble a failed LRU and reassemble a new LRU.

⚠ **WARNING: Important safety instructions!** Before disassembling an LRU in the system, switch it off and disconnect the input electric power cable (AC or DC sources).

## 8.1 Transducer Unit

The transducer unit is mounted below the HMS array, as shown in ![Figure 8: UWT transducers under the HMS array](path_to_image_8). To replace any transducer (projector, hydrophone), the HMS array must be lifted into the ship’s hull to provide access to the transducer unit. This must be done according to the HMS maintenance manual.

### To access and replace a transducer:
1. Locate the transducers installed on a plate secured to the HMS array frame with four bolts.
2. Open the four bolts securing the plate to the array frame to remove the mounting plate.
3. Pull the plate downward carefully to access the upper side of the mounting plate and the transducer assembly.

![Figure 40: Transducer unit mounting plate – top view](path_to_image_40)
Open the four bolts to remove the mounting plate from the array frame and pull the plate carefully downwards to get access to the plate’s upper side.

The following figure shows the transducer unit plate from the underneath.

![Figure 41: Transducer unit mounting plate – Underside view](path_to_image_41)

### 8.1.1 LF Projector

To disassemble the LF projector:
1. Open the six bolts that hold the LF projector mounting flange to remove the LF projector from the mounting plate, as shown in the following figure.

![Figure 42: LF projector with mounting flange](path_to_image_42)
2. Pull the LF projector downwards carefully and disconnect its connector.
3. Disassemble the mounting flange from the LF projector by opening six bolts, as shown in the following figure.

![Figure 43: LF projector – mounting bolts](path_to_image_43)
4. Remove the LF projector and install the new replacement LF projector in the opposite sequence to its removal.

### 8.1.2 HF Projector

To disassemble the HF projector:
1. Open the six bolts that hold the HF projector mounting flange to remove the HF projector from the mounting plate, as shown in the following figure.

![Figure 44: HF projector with mounting flange](path_to_image_44)
2. Pull the HF projector downwards carefully and disconnect its connector.
3. Disassemble the mounting flange from the HF projector by opening six bolts as shown in the above figure.
4. Remove the HF projector and install the new replacement HF projector in the opposite sequence to its removal.

### 8.1.3 Hydrophone

To disassemble the hydrophone:
1. Remove the two bolts that hold the hydrophone to its mounting device, as shown in the following figure.

![Figure 45: Hydrophone mounting device](path_to_image_45)
2. Disconnect the hydrophone connector and remove the hydrophone.
3. Install the new replacement hydrophone in the opposite sequence to its removal.
